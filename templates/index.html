A British School in London for Students Worldwide

London Maths & Science College is a British 16–19 STEM college based in London, welcoming students from the UK and overseas. We deliver the full British curriculum at sixth form level, with A Level and Level 3 BTEC pathways that are recognised by universities and employers around the world.

Whether you study on campus in London, fully online, or in a hybrid model, you follow the same high standards, rigorous programmes of study and clear progression routes that define a high-quality British education.

Study the Full British Curriculum

At LMSC, students follow a structured British curriculum designed to build strong subject knowledge, exam skills and independent study habits. Our programmes prepare you for UK-recognised qualifications, including A Levels and Level 3 BTECs, providing a robust foundation for university entry and professional training.

We focus on subjects that open doors to high-value, future-focused pathways: Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Sciences, Computing, Economics, Business and related disciplines.

Choose How You Study

Every student's situation is different. LMSC offers three study modes, all aligned to the same British curriculum and high expectations.

In-person study at our London campus

Learn in a calm, structured classroom environment with face-to-face teaching, supervised study spaces and support clinics.

  • ✓ Daily lessons in small classes
  • ✓ Quiet study spaces for independent work
  • ✓ In-person mocks, revision sessions and mentoring

Fully online, with live lessons and recordings

Join live British-curriculum lessons from home and watch lesson recordings any time to review explanations or catch up.

  • ✓ Live, timetabled online classes
  • ✓ Full lesson recordings available 24/7
  • ✓ Homework, assessments and feedback via online platform

Hybrid: a flexible blend of campus and online

Combine days on campus with online learning to balance structure, flexibility and family circumstances.

  • ✓ Personalised mix of on-site and online lessons
  • ✓ Option to attend key subjects in person
  • ✓ Same curriculum, assessment and support

Why Choose London Maths & Science College?

LMSC is built for students who want the challenge and prestige of a British sixth form education, with the support and structure to succeed. We combine rigorous teaching, small classes and a clear focus on progression to top universities and future-ready careers.

Specialist 16–19 STEM focus

Our teaching, timetable and enrichment are centred on Maths, Sciences, Computing and Business — subjects that underpin modern, high-value careers.

Small classes and individual attention

We keep class sizes deliberately small so teachers can check understanding, give detailed feedback and quickly address any gaps.

Structured assessment and feedback

Regular quizzes, topic tests and mock exams give students a clear picture of their progress long before final exams.

Clear progression to UK universities

Our programmes are designed around typical entry requirements for British universities, including competitive courses in Medicine, Engineering, Computer Science and Business.

British curriculum, global pathways

Studying the British curriculum in London (or online with a London-based college) helps students open doors in the UK and at universities worldwide.

Calm, supportive learning environment

High expectations are balanced with pastoral care, study skills support and honest communication with families.

For International Students and Families

Many of our students and families are based outside the UK but want the structure, depth and recognition of a British education. Studying with LMSC allows international learners to follow a full British curriculum led by teachers based in London, with clear expectations and regular communication.

  • ✓ Follow British A Level and BTEC programmes recognised worldwide
  • ✓ Study fully online or in a hybrid pattern that fits your time zone and family life
  • ✓ Receive guidance on applying to UK universities and understanding entry requirements

We work closely with families to ensure students understand how British exams, grading and university applications work, so there are no surprises later.

How We Teach at LMSC

Our teaching approach blends traditional academic rigour with modern tools and clear routines, so students know what to expect and how to succeed.

Expert subject specialists

Teachers who know their British exam specifications and how to explain complex ideas clearly.
