Our Teachers & Expert Tutors

At London Maths & Science College (LMSC), your lessons are led by specialist STEM teachers and experienced tutors who know the British curriculum inside out. They combine subject expertise with clear explanations, structured practice and individual support.

Who teaches at London Maths & Science College?

LMSC is a specialist 16–19 STEM college. That means our teachers and tutors are chosen for their depth of subject knowledge and their experience in the British system. Many have taught A Levels, GCSEs and Level 3 BTECs for years, worked as examiners or markers, or supported students into competitive university courses.

Whether you join us on campus in London or online from overseas, you'll be taught by the same calibre of British STEM educators.

Subject specialists

Maths, Further Maths, Sciences, Computing, Economics & Business

British system expertise

Experience with British schools, colleges and online settings

Exam board knowledge

Understanding of UK exam boards and university requirements

Online & in-person

Subject specialists, not generalists

More than lessons: mentoring, guidance and support

Our teachers' role doesn't end when the lesson finishes. They are central to the support and guidance that students receive throughout their time at LMSC.

In smaller classes, teachers get to know each student well — their strengths, their challenges and the most effective way to support them.

Revision & Support

University Guidance

Teachers trained for both in-person and online teaching

Great online teaching is more than pointing a camera at a classroom. Our team are trained to deliver engaging, interactive lessons.

In Person

Use the classroom space, whiteboards and physical resources to model examples and check understanding with immediate feedback.

Online

Use digital whiteboards, screen sharing and chat tools to keep lessons interactive with students answering, voting and working live.

Hybrid

Plan carefully so that remote students feel included and can follow explanations clearly while maintaining engagement.

All modes share the same lesson structure, curriculum, and expectations for participation and effort.

Recruitment, vetting and safeguarding

Because we work with young people, safety and integrity are non-negotiable. Before joining LMSC, teachers and tutors must meet strict checks, including:

✓

Relevant qualifications and experience

In their subject and in education

✓

Background checks

Compliant with UK safeguarding requirements (enhanced DBS or equivalent)

✓

Professional references

From trusted educational organisations

We also train staff in safeguarding, child protection and online safety, and we expect them to uphold the college's values of respect, inclusion and professionalism at all times.

Teachers as partners with parents and guardians

We see parents and guardians as partners in each student's success. Our teachers and tutors play a central role in this relationship.

  • ✓ Regular updates on progress, strengths and areas for improvement
  • ✓ Contribution to written reports and progress review meetings
  • ✓ Contact when there are concerns about effort, attendance or wellbeing
  • ✓ Practical advice on how families can support study routines at home

We aim for communication that is honest, clear and respectful, focused on what will help the student move forward.
