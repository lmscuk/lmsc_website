Why London Maths & Science College?

London Maths & Science College (LMSC) is a British 16-19 STEM college based in London. We deliver the full British curriculum through A Levels and Level 3 BTECs in Maths, Sciences, Computing and Business, with in-person, online and hybrid study options for students in the UK and worldwide.

A British sixth form college for a global generation

London Maths & Science College was created for students who want the academic rigour and recognition of a British sixth form education, combined with the flexibility of modern study modes.

We specialise exclusively in post-16 education. That means our timetable, teaching and support are all focused on helping 16-19 year olds succeed in demanding subjects and progress to strong destinations -- particularly in STEM and related fields.

Whether a student joins us on campus in London or studies fully online from another country, they follow the same British curriculum, the same schemes of work and the same high expectations.

British curriculum, taught from London

Study with us in the way that works for you

LMSC offers three main study modes, all aligned to the same curriculum and standards:

In person (London campus)

Small, structured classes on campus, supervised study spaces and in-person support clinics.

Fully online

Hybrid

A blended pattern that combines days on campus with online learning, agreed with each family to balance structure and flexibility.
