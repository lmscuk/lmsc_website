Online & In-Person Study Options

At London Maths & Science College, you can study the full British 16-19 curriculum in the way that works best for you—on campus in London, fully online from home, or through a flexible hybrid route. Whichever option you choose, you follow the same British A Level and BTEC programmes, taught from London by specialist teachers.

Information for home-educating families

One British curriculum, three ways to study

All of our students, wherever they are in the world, follow the same British curriculum, with aligned schemes of work, assessments and expectations. The difference lies in how you access that learning: in person at our London campus, online from home, or a blend of the two.

For many families—especially internationally or in home-education contexts—this flexibility makes it possible to access a rigorous British sixth form education without relocating or compromising on other commitments.

In-person study at our London campus

For students who thrive in a structured classroom environment, our London campus provides a calm, focused setting with clear routines and face-to-face support.

  • Small classes with live teaching every lesson
  • Access to study spaces, supervised revision and on-site support clinics
    • In-person assessments and mock exams under exam-style conditions
  • Daily contact with teachers and peers in a British college environment

In-person study is ideal if you live within reach of London and want the full experience of attending a British sixth form college day to day.

Fully online: British curriculum from home

Our fully online programmes are designed for students who need flexibility, but still want the structure and recognition of a British A Level or BTEC programme taught from London.

  • Live, timetabled lessons delivered via our online classroom
  • Lesson recordings available 24/7 so you can revisit explanations or catch up
  • Digital resources, homework and assessments set and submitted online
  • Regular progress checks, feedback and individual guidance

Online students follow the same British curriculum as on-campus learners, with teachers based in London and a clear timetable to keep learning on track.

Hybrid study: combining campus and online learning

Hybrid study is ideal for students who want some of the structure and social life of campus, but also need the flexibility of online learning—for example due to travel, health, family or training commitments.

Study options for home-educating families

Many of our online learners are part of home-educating families who want the depth and recognition of a British curriculum alongside the flexibility of learning at home.

LMSC can support home education by:

  • Providing structured A Level, GCSE and BTEC programmes with clear schemes of work
  • Offering live lessons and recorded content to anchor your home timetable
  • Advising on exam entries, predicted grades and university applications
  • Ensuring your child's progress is tracked and communicated clearly

You remain fully in control as a home-educating parent, while we provide the academic spine and expert teaching.
